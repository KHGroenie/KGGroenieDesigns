What I Need From You

Content checklist

Please send all of the following before I begin. Missing content delays your project.

Business name and tagline (what you do in one sentence)
Your logo file (PNG or SVG with transparent background preferred)
Brand colours if you have them (hex codes or just describe the vibe)
Photos — minimum 3, ideally 5–8 (see Photo Guidelines below)
About section — 2–3 sentences about your business or yourself

Your contact details (phone, email, WhatsApp, address if relevant)
Social media links (Facebook, Instagram, etc.)
Any specific pages or sections you want included
Examples of websites you like the look of (optional but helpful)

Important: The clock on your 5–7 day delivery only starts once I have received ALL of the above. Incomplete briefs delay your launch.

Photos

Photo guidelines

Photos make or break a website. Please take a moment to read this before sending images.

✓ Do

  • Use good natural lighting
  • Send high resolution (taken on a modern phone is fine)
  • Show your actual work, products, or space
  • Keep backgrounds clean and uncluttered
  • Send landscape (horizontal) photos where possible
  • Send originals — don't crop or compress first

✗ Don't

  • Send blurry or dark photos
  • Send screenshots of photos
  • Use heavy filters
  • Send photos with watermarks
  • Send very small or compressed images
  • Use photos you don't own the rights to

No good photos?

Let me know and I can source high-quality free stock photos that suit your business. Just describe your industry and vibe.

Domain Name

Buying your domain

Your domain name (e.g. yourbusiness.co.za) is purchased separately by you. It typically costs R150–R300 per year.

Recommended registrars in South Africa:

Afrihost — afrihost.com — popular and reliable
Xneelo — xneelo.co.za — good local support
GoDaddy — godaddy.com — often has deals

Once purchased: Create a free Netlify account at netlify.com using your business email. Send me your login details and I will connect your domain to your site.

After Launch

Getting support

Once your site is live, all change requests and questions go through the support form on my website. This creates a ticket so nothing gets missed.

1

Visit the support form

Go to my website and click "Submit a Request" — takes 2 minutes to fill in.

2

Describe what you need

Be specific — e.g. "Change my phone number from X to Y" or "Add a new photo to the gallery."

3

I'll confirm and action it

You'll get a confirmation and I'll aim to action it within 2–3 working days.

Remember

Your first 3 changes after launch are free. From change 4 onwards, each change is R500. Changes are counted per request, not per edit within a request — so group them together where you can.
